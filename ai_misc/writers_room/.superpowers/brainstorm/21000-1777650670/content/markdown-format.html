## Personality
Optimistic, quick-witted, and fiercely loyal to his friends. Acts before thinking, often landing himself in deeper trouble while trying to fix things.

## Voice Pattern
Uses 80s slang liberally. "This is heavy." "Are you telling me..." Often starts sentences with "Wait a minute." Trails off in disbelief.

## Character Arc
From reckless risk-taker who acts without thinking to someone who understands that every action has consequences — across time and relationships.

## Motivation
Get home to 1985 intact. Protect his family without destroying their future.

## Internal Conflict
Torn between the instinct to fix everything immediately and the hard lesson that some things must unfold on their own.

## Physical Description
Teenager, wiry and energetic. Red puffy vest, blue jeans, white sneakers. Never still — always in motion.

## Mannerisms
Pulls on his ear when nervous. Double-takes at impossible things. Skateboard stance even when standing still.

He's the only kid ever to get into trouble before he was born.

A teenager accidentally travels 30 years into the past and must get his parents to fall in love or he'll never be born.

In 1985 Hill Valley, Marty McFly is inadvertently sent back to 1955 in a time-travelling DeLorean...

Hill Valley, California — 1985 and 1955. Small-town America at two generational extremes.

- Must orchestrate their romance while finding a way back

Marty vs. Biff (external bully). Marty vs. paradox (existential). Marty vs. his own impatience (internal).

1955 Hill Valley culture, music, fashion. Twin Pines Mall / Lone Pine Mall continuity.

Convention

YAML frontmatter holds structured/typed fields (enums, foreign key slugs, lists, model names).
Markdown ## sections hold all narrative prose (personality, arcs, context, atmosphere).
Section headers match database column names exactly — making import/export deterministic with no custom mapping needed.
The existing teen_play project YAML files can be migrated to this format on first import.
